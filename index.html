About Me

I have a bachelors degree in Mechanical Engineering from the Institute of Engineering, Pulchowk Campus, Tribhuvan University. Throughout my undergraduate, I gained experience in Mechanical Design and Simulation, specially with Engineering design and simulation software like SOLIDWORKS, ANSYS, and OpenFOAM. In addition, I also gained experience with robotics working as a core team member of Robotics Club. I worked for developing robots for ABU Robocon Competitions and participated in-person in the competition along with teams from 20 other nations, in Vietnam in 2018, and in Mongolia in 2019.

Furthermore, I have a keen interest in Biotech and Product Development Industries. My previous experience as a Product Development Engineer allowed me to work on the mechanical design and simulation of electric scooters, where I also learned about the technical and managerial aspects of product developement. This experience, combined with my passion for developing technologies that can improve the lives of people with disabilities, has motivated me to pursue my graduate studies in Mechanical Engineering with a focus in BioMechanics.
